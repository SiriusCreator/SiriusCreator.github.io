Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize a template? Select a template from the app’s library, then use the editing tools to add photos, text, stickers, or emojis. Adjust fonts, colors, and sizes to personalize your card.

Can I use the app offline? Yes, once set up, the app works offline for creating and saving cards. An internet connection is needed for sharing or accessing new templates.

How do I share my card? Save your card and share it via WhatsApp, email, social media, or print it directly from your device.

What if I encounter an issue? Check our troubleshooting section below or contact us at siriuscreator999@gmail.com.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes: Ensure your device meets the app’s requirements (Android/iOS latest versions). Clear the app cache or reinstall if issues persist.

Sharing Issues: Verify your internet connection and ensure the recipient’s platform supports the file format (e.g., JPEG, PNG).

Template Loading: Check for app updates in the App Store, as new templates are added regularly.
