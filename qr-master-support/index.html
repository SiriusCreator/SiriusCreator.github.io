About the App

QR Master allows you to scan any QR code or barcode instantly using your camera or by importing images. You can also generate custom QR codes for URLs, Wi-Fi networks, contacts, and text. With history tracking and batch scanning features, managing your data has never been easier.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom QR code? Navigate to the "Create" section, select the type of data (URL, Text, Wi-Fi, etc.), enter your information, and tap "Generate." You can then save or share your new code.

The app isn't scanning. What should I do? Ensure your camera lens is clean and that there is sufficient lighting. Hold your device steady and align the code within the on-screen viewfinder.

Where can I find my previous scans? All scanned and generated codes are automatically stored in the "History" tab for easy access later.

Can I scan barcodes on products? Yes, QR Master supports all major barcode formats (UPC, EAN, Code 128, etc.) to help you look up product information.

Troubleshooting

Camera Permission: If the scanner shows a black screen, please check your device settings to ensure QR Master has permission to access your camera.

Flashlight Support: For scanning in dark environments, use the flashlight icon on the scanning screen to improve visibility.

App Updates: Ensure you are running the latest version of QR Master from the store to receive the newest scanning algorithms and bug fixes.
